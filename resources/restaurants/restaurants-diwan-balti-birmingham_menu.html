Welcome to Diwan Balti! Our restaurant here in the heart of the Balti Triangle in Birmingham specializes in, yep, you guessed it, Indian cuisine & damn good Indian at that. We are passionate about serving up quality, delicious & authentic fare & have an extensive menu with all your favourite classics, as well as some signature specialities we’re proud of. We’re one of the three original balti restaurants in Birmingham & our team go above & beyond to make sure you have a meal to remember each & every time. Why not try our Prime Minister Special? Its what ex-PM David Cameron ate when he visited our restaurant.
