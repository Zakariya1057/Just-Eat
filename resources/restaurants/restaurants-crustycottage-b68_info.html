Description

The award winning Crusty Cottage has expanded to Oakley Gardens on Dial Lane, where we will continue to provide our highly rated service to new customers. Great customer service is something we pride ourselves in and we have been awarded Tried and Tasted two years in a row. These high levels of service will be continued in our additional restaurant at Oakley Gardens, where we will be able to reach out and deliver to new lovely customers in new areas. Why not browse through our menu as we can ensure you that you will find something to tickle your taste buds! Treat yourself to our much loved milkshakes or our well known Mega Crusty Cottage Breakfast. Also don’t forget to leave us a review! Thank You

Crusty Cottage is a well-established business which is located on Bleakhouse Road in Oldbury. Our great customer service is something we pride ourselves in, and this is shown by our excellent reviews. We are also described as ‘tried and tested’, therefore ensuring our customers that the food quality will always remain high.

We pride ourselves in our achievements for Food and Hygiene as we have been awarded the highest Level 5 for food and Hygiene which can be found at http://ratings.food.gov.uk/ . As well as this, our staff have the relevant food and hygiene certificates.

Why not browse through our menu as we can ensure you that you will find something to tickle your taste buds! Treat yourself to our much loved milkshakes or try the new editions to our menu which includes paninis and toasties.

Also don’t forget to leave us a review!
